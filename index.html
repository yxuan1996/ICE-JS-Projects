Ice-js-projects

Demo of several projects that I made using the ICE GamingJS Editor.

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

Spinning Donut

An Animation of a Spinning Donut. I played around with different materials, lightning and shadows. The shadow of the donut changes according to position of the donut. Spinning Donut

Fruit Hunt

A cute little game. Move your character around and grab the fruit from the shaking trees using your spacebar. Fruit Hunt

Planets

A View of the Sun, the Earth and Mars. Press A for Solar System View. Press E for View of Mars from Earth. Learnt about the Particle Generator System which was used to create the stars. Planets

Moon Phases

Check out the different phases of the moon! Press C or spacebar to switch views. Press P to pause the simulation. Use 1,2,3 to control the speed of the animation. Moon Phases

Purple Fruit Monster

You are the Purple Fruit Monster and your objective is to catch as many fruits as possible without touching the ground! Arrow keys to move and spacebar to jump. R to reset the game. Purple Fruit Monster

Tilt a Board

Make sure that the ball falls into the middle of the hashtag shaped board! Arrow keys to tilt the board. Tilt a Board

Multi-level cave puzzle

Help the ball get out of the cave by positioning the ramps so that the ball can roll up. Left and right arrow keys to control the ball. Use the mouse to position the ramps. Press S to rotate the ramps. Since this is a multi-level game, the next level, which contains more obstacles, will start immediately after you complete the current level. Can you complete all of the levels in time? Multi Level Cave Puzzle

River Rafting

Steer your raft down the river using arrow keys. The tiny circle in front of the raft indicates the front of the raft. Press Spacebar to accelerate forward. Look out for floating fruits on the river, they can provide a HUGE ammount of bonus points! River Rafting
